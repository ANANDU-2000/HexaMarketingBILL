## 3. Three-Day Aggressive Action Plan

**Goal:** Maximum direct outreach in 72 hours. SEO will not rank in 3 days; focus is demos and WhatsApp.

### Day 1

- **Google Maps scraping strategy:** Search “distributor Dubai”, “wholesale FMCG UAE”, “frozen food distributor UAE”, “ice trading Dubai”. Export or manually list company name, area, phone, and (if visible) contact. Target: 50–80 companies. Add to contact tracker.
- **LinkedIn DM script:** Use script in Section 4 (first message). Send to owners, GMs, and “Head of Sales” or “Finance” at distributors. Target: 30–50 DMs.
- **WhatsApp cold message script:** Use Section 4 first message. Only to numbers you have (from Maps, website, or referral). Target: 40–60 first messages.
- **60-second Loom script:** “Hi, I’m [name] from HexaBill. We build billing software for Gulf distributors — VAT, credit, routes, branches. In 60 seconds: [screen share] here’s the dashboard, aging report, and route P&L. If you want a demo login to click through yourself, reply here or WhatsApp [number]. Thanks.” Record once; reuse link in follow-ups.

### Day 2

- **Niche Facebook groups:** Join UAE/GCC business, SME, distributor, and FMCG groups. Post value-first (e.g. “3 things distributors get wrong with VAT invoicing”) and in comments offer demo when relevant. No hard spam. Target: 5–10 groups, 2–3 helpful posts.
- **Distributor associations:** Identify chambers, trade bodies, and distributor associations in UAE/KSA/Qatar. Email or contact form with cold email template (Section 4). Ask for intro or to share with members. Target: 5–10 associations.
- **Cold email template:** Use template in Section 4. Send to emails from Maps/website. Target: 50–80 emails.
- **Referral activation:** Message existing 3 clients: “We’re doing a short push for new Gulf distributors. If you know one owner or GM who’s struggling with credit or VAT, can you intro us? We’ll give them a demo same day.” Target: 3 personal asks, 1–2 intros if possible.

### Day 3

- **Urgency offer:** “This week we’re offering [X] — [e.g. waived onboarding / first month discount / free setup]. Demo first, then we apply it if you sign by [date].” Use in WhatsApp and email.
- **Limited-time onboarding discount:** Define one clear offer (e.g. 20% off first 3 months or free onboarding). State expiry (e.g. “Offer valid for demos booked by [date]”). Use only after they’ve had the demo.
- **Close script:** “You’ve seen the demo. What’s the one thing that would make you comfortable moving forward — price, timing, or something in the product?” Then address that. “If we do [X], can you start by [date]?”
- **Objection handling:** Price: “We’re priced for SMB distributors; compare cost vs. your current Excel + mistakes + FTA risk.” Timing: “Demo is 15 minutes; we can do it today and you decide when to switch.” Already have software: “We’re built for distribution; worth a 15-min look to see if it’s a fit.” No reply: Use Follow-up 2 and then one last short “Should I close your file or keep you on the list?” message.

## 9. Pricing Psychology Strategy

- **Anchor pricing:** Show the higher plan (Pro or Enterprise) first or next to Starter so the mid plan feels like the “sensible” choice. Example: Pro as default, “Most Gulf distributors choose Pro.”
- **Starter vs Pro positioning:** Starter = single branch, basic invoicing and VAT. Pro = routes, branches, credit limits, aging. Frame Pro as “for anyone with more than one route or branch.”
- **Usage-based add-on:** Optional add-ons (e.g. extra branches, extra users, or premium support) so base price stays low and expansion is clear.
- **Limited offer strategy:** Use only after demo: “We’re offering [X] for demos completed this week” (e.g. waived onboarding or first month discount). Always tie to a deadline and to having seen the demo.

## 10. Realistic 3-Day Expectations

**What can actually happen in 3 days**

- You can send 100–300 personalised touches (WhatsApp, email, LinkedIn, referral asks).
- You can get 5–20 replies and 2–8 demos sent.
- You may get 0–2 “we want to move forward” signals. Closing 1 paying client in 3 days is a strong outcome.

**Realistic close rate**

- Cold outreach to SMB owners: 1–3% reply rate; of those, 10–25% take a demo; of demos, 10–30% convert to paid within 2–4 weeks. So: 100 contacts → ~2–5 demos → 0–1 quick close. 200–300 contacts improve odds.

**How many companies to contact**

- To have a realistic shot at 1 close in 3 days: contact 100–300 companies (mix of WhatsApp, email, LinkedIn, referrals). More volume = more demos = more chance of one fast “yes.”

**Conversion % to expect**

- Replies: 1–5%. Demo requests: 0.5–2%. Signed client in 3 days: 0.3–1% of contacted. SEO will not move in 3 days; this playbook is for direct outreach and WhatsApp conversion only.
