# 30-Day Client Acquisition Plan

Context: WhatsApp-heavy Gulf market, distributor niche (frozen, FMCG, ice, wholesale), referral chain working (3 clients). Goal: demos and signed clients over 30 days, not brand awareness.

---

**WhatsApp (primary)**  
Use [GULF_GROWTH_PLAYBOOK.md](GULF_GROWTH_PLAYBOOK.md) Section 4: First message, Follow-up 1, Follow-up 2, Close message. Short, respectful, demo-focused.

**Cold email variant (same intent)**

Body: Hi [Name], I’m [Your name] from HexaBill. We make billing software for UAE/GCC distributors — VAT, credit aging, routes, branches. We send a demo login so you can see the screens yourself; no commitment. If you’d like it, reply with your WhatsApp or say “Yes” and I’ll send the link. We reply within 2 hours on UAE business days. Thanks.

**LinkedIn DM variant**  
Same as WhatsApp first message: who you are, what HexaBill is (billing for distributors — VAT, credit, routes), offer of demo login, no commitment. Ask if they’re open to receiving it. Keep under 150 words.

## Demo Positioning Script

When someone says “send me more info” or “tell me more”:

“We don’t send a long PDF — we send you a demo login. You log in, click around, and see the dashboard, credit aging, VAT, and route screens yourself. Takes 2 minutes to set up. After that, if you want a short call to go through your specific setup (routes, branches), we do that. Most people prefer to look first, then we talk. Want me to send the demo ID?”

Use this in WhatsApp, email, or call. Keeps the next step clear: demo first, then conversation.

---

## Close Script

After they’ve seen the demo (or had a walkthrough):

“Thanks for checking the demo. What’s the one thing that would make you comfortable moving forward — price, onboarding, or something in the product?”

Listen. Then:

“If we do [X] — [e.g. waive onboarding / first month discount / add Y feature], can you start by [date]?”

If they need to think: “No problem. When should I check back — this week or next?”

Do not leave without a clear next step or a date to follow up.

## Objection Handling

**Price**  
“We’re priced for SMB distributors. Compare the cost to your current setup: Excel errors, FTA risk, and time chasing credit. Most owners see the payback in a few months. We can also start with one branch and add more when you’re ready.”

**Timing / “We’re busy”**  
“Demo is 15 minutes. We send the login; you look when it suits you. You decide when to switch. No rush — but if you want the link today, I can send it now.”

**“We have Zoho / Tally / QuickBooks”**  
“Those are strong for general invoicing and accounting. HexaBill is built for distribution: route sales, branch P&L, and credit limits out of the box. Worth a 15-minute look to see if it’s a fit for how you run routes and branches. No commitment.”

**“Just send me information”**  
Use the Demo Positioning Script above. “We don’t send a long PDF — we send a demo login so you can see it yourself.”

**No reply after 2 follow-ups**  
One last message: “Should I close your file or keep you on the list for when timing is better? No problem either way.” Then stop. Mark as Lost in tracker.

## Referral Multiplier Plan

**Who to ask**  
Existing 3 clients. Best time: after a support win, successful go-live, or first month with no issues.

**Script**  
“We’re looking to work with a few more Gulf distributors like you. If you know one owner or GM who’s struggling with credit, VAT, or route visibility — would you be comfortable introducing us? We’ll give them a demo same day and won’t spam them. One intro is enough; we’ll take it from there.”

**When to ask**  
- Once per client per quarter, max.  
- After you’ve delivered something they value (fix, feature, or smooth onboarding).

**How to track**  
In your contact spreadsheet (see [CONTACT_EXECUTION_SPREADSHEET_SPEC.md](CONTACT_EXECUTION_SPREADSHEET_SPEC.md)): set Source = “Referral” and in Notes write “Intro from [Client name]”. Follow up with the referred lead within 2 hours.

**Goal**  
1–2 intros from 3 clients in 30 days is a strong outcome. Don’t push for more; quality of intro matters more than volume.

---

## 30-Day Rhythm (high level)

- **Weeks 1–2:** Volume. 100–150 cold touches (WhatsApp, email, LinkedIn). Send demos to everyone who says yes. Use Demo Positioning Script when they ask for “info.”
- **Weeks 2–3:** Follow-ups. F-up 1 and F-up 2 for non-replies. Close script for everyone who saw the demo. Ask 3 clients for one referral each (using script above).
- **Week 4:** Close and urgency. “We’re offering [X] for demos completed this week” if you have a limited offer. Use Close Script and objection handling. Update tracker: Demo sent, Closed, Lost.

**Realistic 30-day outcome:** 5–15 demos sent, 1–3 “moving forward” signals, 0–1 signed client. If you hit 1 signed client from cold + referral, that’s a win. Adjust volume and follow-up discipline based on reply rates.
