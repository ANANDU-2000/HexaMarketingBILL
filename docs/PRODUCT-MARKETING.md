# HexaBill Marketing — Product Marketing

Positioning, messaging, and feature framing for the marketing site. **For agents:** Read this before writing or editing hero, pain, solution, or feature copy.

## Positioning

- **Audience:** Business owners and GMs of B2B distributors and wholesalers in the UAE and Gulf (not IT or technical buyers).
- **Owner cares about:** Money, time, and control. Messaging should speak to revenue, cash flow, time saved, and visibility/control (who owes what, stock, routes, profit).
- **Product:** HexaBill = Distributor ERP / Billing SaaS. Billing & inventory software for UAE distributors; one system for sales, inventory, customers, and payments.

## Messaging

- **Hero:** "Billing & Inventory Software for UAE Distributors" / "Manage sales, inventory, customers, and payments in one system."
- **Solution line:** "HexaBill automates your billing, inventory, and reports."
- **Trust:** "We work with distributors in the Gulf"; "Reply within 2 hours on WhatsApp"; "Demo login so you can see it before you decide."
- **Avoid:** Technical jargon in headlines; "free trial" (we offer demo login instead); overclaiming scale.

## Feature framing

- **8 owner-focused modules:** POS Billing, Inventory Management, Supplier Management, Customer Ledger, Route Sales System, Reports & Profit, VAT Compliance (UAE), Invoice PDF & Email.
- **Copy:** Lead with outcome (e.g. "Create invoices in seconds", "See who owes you", "Track stock automatically"). Bullets = benefits, not implementation details.
- **Screenshots:** Each feature should have a clear screenshot idea (e.g. POS cart, product list with stock, ledger table, route performance, reports dashboard). See `docs/SCREENSHOTS-ASSETS.md`.

## Competitor differentiation

- **vs Excel:** One system, no version chaos; auto VAT; real-time ledger and reports.
- **vs generic invoicing:** Built for distributors: routes, branches, credit ageing, supplier ledger, FTA VAT.
- **vs "free trial" SaaS:** Honest demo login; no fake trial claims; WhatsApp-first for Gulf.
